Welcome

I am an Assistant Professor at SUNY Binghamton. My research interests span operating systems, systems virtualization, file and storage systems, computer networks, and systems performance optimization, especially in the context of cloud computing. Before joining SUNY Binghamton, I received my Ph.D. degree from Purdue University, where I researched in FRIENDS Lab under the direction of Professor Dongyan Xu.

I am the recipient of an NSF CAREER Award (2023).
